Oatmeal Muffin Recipe

Time and servings

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Servings: 12 muffins

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Muffin Pan
  • Paper liners
  • Oven

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 1/4 tsp of baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp of baking
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups oats
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/3 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
    • Buy ingredients on Amazon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Line a 12-cup muffin pan with paper liners.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt.
  4. In a different bowl stir the oats and milk, let stand for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the brown sugar, oil, and eggs. Stir to combine. Divide the batter among the paper liners.(sprinkle oats on top if desired).
  6. Bake for 7 minutes, Reduce oven temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  7. Let muffins cool in pan for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Enjoy!
